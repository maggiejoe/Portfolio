About Me

Hey! Welcome to my portfolio! If you're new here, my name's Maggie and I'm so happy you're here taking the time to look through my work. I'm an aspiring full stack developer currently going through the Coding BootCamp with the University of Toronto. I decided I wanted to get into web development when I realized my current career path in retail really wasn't able to give me the fulfilling life I was looking for. While browsing my options I stumbled upon this BootCamp and the rest is history! Once this program is complete and my knowledge grows (through this program and beyond - we all know this industry requires developers to be very responsive to the continuous changing and updating of languages), I plan on doing remote or freelance work. It has always been my goal to travel the world and to have a career that I can bring on the road with me, while also giving me creative freedom and web development seems to check all of those boxes! Thank you so much for visiting and I can't wait to connect with you!
